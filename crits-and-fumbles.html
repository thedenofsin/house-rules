Critical Hit Table — All Weapons & Magic

  • A critical hit always deals maximum normal damage.
  • Double Damage (DD) and Triple Damage (TD) always deal a minimum of MAX damage.
  • 00 is Immediate death for all categories.
  • Anatomy rule: If the listed body part/condition doesn’t apply, ignore the effect and apply damage only (MAX/DD/TD).
  • All monsters (humanoid or not) use the Semi-martial column by default.
